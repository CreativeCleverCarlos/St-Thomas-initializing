    Giving



    If you are interested in contributing towards our church, please select one of the 3 options below

    E-Transfer


    Make your donation to the Bramalea Church of Christ by sending an e-transfer to us. Contact us to learn more about this way of giving. Donations are tax receiptable.

    In-Person


    At any time, you are welcome to drop off donations at the building, or you can give your donation on a Sunday morning during the collection time.

    Cheque


    Take advantage of setting up automatic withdrawals by completing this form and attaching a VOID cheque. Then just mail us both documents to our church address and we’ll get you all setup. It’s that simple to do!
